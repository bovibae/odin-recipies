Lasagna

This lasagna dish is made vegetarian by swapping out meat for a plant-based meat alternative

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 4 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1 lb impossible ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup parsley
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 36 ounces pasta sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F. Cook lasagna noodles until al dente. Drain, rinse under cold water, and set aside
  2. In a large pan, sautee the beef, chopped onion, and minced garlic.
  3. Stir in the pasta sauce, tomato paste, italian seasoning, salt and pepper to taste. Simmer over medium heat for 5 minutes
  4. In a separate bowl, combine 1 1/2 cups mozarella, 1/4 cup parm, ricotta, parsley, egg, and a dash of salt
  5. Spread 1 cup of the meat sauce in a casserole dish. Top it with 3 lasagna noodles. Layer with 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mix and one cup of meat sauce. Repeat twice more. Finish with 3 lasagna noodles topped with the remaining sauce
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 45 min
  7. Remove foil, sprinkle remaining mozzarella and parm. Bake for an additional 15 minutes or until browned and bubbly
  8. Rest for 15 minutes. Enjoy!
