Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Jelly of choice
  • Peanut butter
  1. Toast the slices of bread
  2. Spread jelly on one slice of bread
  3. Spread peanut butter on the other slice
  4. Place one slice on top of the other. The sides with peanut butter and jelly should touch
